Privacy Policy for JellyJumper: Infinite Jumps

Effective Date: 29.01.2025

Thank you for playing JellyJumper: Infinite Jumps! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

JellyJumper: Infinite Jumps does not collect, store, or track any personal data, usage statistics, or behavioral analytics. The only integration present in the game is with Apple's Game Center, which is subject to Apple's own privacy policies.

2. Game Center Integration

JellyJumper: Infinite Jumps utilizes Apple's Game Center for achievements and leaderboards. If you choose to sign in with Game Center, Apple may collect and process data according to their own policies. We do not access, store, or manage any Game Center data ourselves.

For more information, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy

3. No Data Collection

We do not:

  • Collect personal data
  • Track user behavior
  • Use third-party analytics or advertising services
  • Share or sell any user data

4. Children's Privacy

JellyJumper: Infinite Jumps does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of exposure to personal information.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the game or on our official website. Continued use of the game after modifications constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
